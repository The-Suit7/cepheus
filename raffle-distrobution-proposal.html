1. Executive Summary

This proposal introduces a ticket-based raffle system to replace traditional ISK auctions for distributing high-value null sec assets. The system rewards fleet participation, ensures fair access for all corps, generates additional alliance revenue, and maintains full transparency through hidden bidding and public audits.

Authored by Benedict Lith of Cepheus. — designed to strengthen alliance cohesion and reward active contributors.

3. Problems with Current ISK Auction System

  • Pay-to-win: Large corps and ISK whales dominate
  • No reward for effort: Fleet participation is ignored
  • Excludes small/new corps: Unequal access to valuable assets
  • Low motivation: No direct link between PVP and rewards

4. The Solution: Ticket-Based Raffle System

Core Mechanics

  • Tickets are the currency for entering raffles
  • Earn tickets: 1 ticket per qualifying fleet participation
  • Buy tickets: 20,000,000 ISK each (non-refundable, 100% profit)
  • One-time use: Tickets assigned unique ID when bid; cannot be reused
  • Winner pays base cost in ISK after draw

Ticket Purchase Limits

  • Each corporation may purchase a maximum of 200 ISK tickets per raffle cycle.
  • Earned tickets (from fleet participation) are unlimited.
  • Purpose: Prevents ISK whales from dominating while preserving effort-based access.
Tickets have no cash value — they only grant a chance to win the right to purchase the asset.

5. Bidding & Drawing Process

  1. Corps earn and/or purchase tickets
  2. Submit bids (number of tickets per item) — hidden from others
  3. RNG draw using public, verifiable random number generator
  4. Reveal all bids for full audit and transparency
  5. Winner pays base cost in ISK to claim the structure

9. Key Benefits

  • Rewards fleet participation — PVP effort directly translates to opportunity
  • Inclusive — small corps can compete through activity
  • Fair odds — win chance proportional to total investment (effort + ISK)
  • Transparent — hidden bids + public draw + full audit
  • Engaging — lottery excitement boosts morale and participation
  • Profitable — generates surplus ISK for alliance goals
