Cepheus Combat

Cepheus enjoys space combat. A camaraderie culture means members got your back in combat at times of need, and defend Alliance and Coalition space. We provide a ship replacement program in addition to the alliance ship replacement program that most of the time results in more isk paid for your ship loss then it is worth. Official fleets participation rewards that pay members for being in official Corp, Alliance, or Coalition fleets that earn you a PAP. Contracts of fleet ships are always ready for the quick grab and go. Custom orders can be made to our Industry team to supply you with everything you need to get out there.
