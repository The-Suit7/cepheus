Rules

Hey pilot, let’s keep the fleet running smooth and avoid mid-battle chaos! Cepheus has some key guidelines to ensure we all thrive in this wild null-sec life. Strap in for the rundown:

  • Be Able to Afford a Loss
    • Never undock in a ship (or with cargo) you can’t afford to lose—even in hi-sec! Fly only what you can replace and refit yourself.
    • Don’t grant corp rights to assets you’d cry over losing—no exceptions, keep your hangar secure!
  • Consent to PvP
    • Clicking "undock" means you’re fair game for a fight—PvP is the name of the game!
    • Don’t expect CONCORD to save you in 1.0 space; they punish, not protect—get comfy with that reality.
    • 100% safety? Dock up in a station or cloak at a secret safespot—anything else is a gamble!
  • Everyone Can Scam - And So Can You
    • Free stuff offers? Likely traps—stay sharp and skeptical.
    • Even your mined minerals aren’t truly "free"—watch for hidden costs.
    • If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is—paranoia is your friend, double-check everything!
    • Scamming and some "griefing" are baked into EVE’s mechanics and even rewarded—go for it if you dare! But never scam or grief corp members or blues—keep the family clean!
    • Harassment or real-life threats? That’s a ban-worthy line—know the difference and keep it clean.
  • Experience Matters, Not ISK or Skills
    • A fresh 5-minute-old alt could be a vet—many run multiple toons, while year-old newbies pop up too.
    • Skills under 1 day to train are short; over a week is long—plan your grind accordingly.
    • This is a long-haul game—don’t expect to rule New Eden on day one of your trial account.
    • Numbers often beat ship value in a fight, but experience and skill levels trump all—2 mil SP combat newbs can smash 60 mil SP industrial vets!
  • Bigger Is Not Always Better
    • Expensive ships aren’t always superior—choose wisely!
    • Better gear costs way more than slightly worse stuff—balance your fit.
    • Tech-2 can be cheaper and stronger but harder to fit—sometimes it’s neither, so test it out.
    • No "solopwnmobiles" exist—everything blows up with enough DPS!
    • With skill and experience, ship size barely matters—fly what fits your style.
  • Use the In-Game Info
    • Master "Show Info" and "Variants → Compare"—check every module and ship before undocking.
    • Insure your hull pre-undock, though T2 ships? Eh, less critical.
  • Unfair Circumstances?
    • No such thing as a "fair fight"—it’s just a fight, circumstances be damned!
    • You can fly it, but should you? Think twice before undocking that shiny new cap.
  • You Will Lose Stuff, Don’t Worry!
    • PvP long enough, and your ship’s toast—it’s a when, not if, deal.
    • Logoff in space? Safe after 1 minute without a PvP timer, 15 with one—don’t bail mid-fight, you’ll just die anyway!
    • Someone out there has better skills, more mates, or more uptime—hope they’re not hunting you!
    • Lost your gear? It’s almost always your call—own it and learn, we’ve got your back to rebound!
  • No Blue Killing
    • Blues are family—don't shoot 'em! If you've got beef with a blue, bring it to corp leadership for resolution, don't take matters into your own pod.
  • Keep Operations Local
    • Stick close to home for most ops, ideally in our core system, so we can back each other up fast and defend our Sov like a well-oiled fleet.
  • Join Discord
    • Joining our Discord is mandatory for comms and intel—highly encouraged to hop in whenever you’re online in-game to stay in the loop and chat with the crew!
  • Having Fun Is The Most Important Part
    • Remember, EVE's all about the thrills—PvP brawls, ISK hauls, or chilling on comms. If it's not fun, let's chat and make it right!

ISK Earning

Let’s stack that wallet, pilot! Cepheus offers a smorgasbord of ISK-making gigs in null-sec, with payouts from 50-200m/hour depending on your fit. Here’s the lineup:

  • Ratting: Blast NPCs in anomalies or belts with a VNI or Ishtar—bounties can hit 100m/hour with escalations.
  • Mining: Pilot a Procurer or Hulk to mine ore or moon goo—Rorqual boosts push yields sky-high!
  • Exploration: Scan relics and data sites with a cloaky Astero—score 100m+/hour from gas or sleeper loot.
  • Salvaging: Trail fleets or ratters with a Noctis for modules and salvage—quick ISK for newbros.
  • ESS Robbing: Snag bounty payouts from enemy ESS with a fast frigate—high risk, high reward up to 200m+.
  • Planetary Interactions (PI): Set up planets for P4 goods—passive 50m/month per character.
  • Manufacturing: Build T2 mods or caps in our complexes—use buyback minerals for profit.
  • Wormhole Space: Day-trip WHs for sleeper sites or gas—200m/hour with good intel.
  • Supplying the Local Market: Flip contracts or export loot to Jita—steady ISK from fleet fits.
  • Loot from PvP: Salvage wrecks post-brawl for T2 salvage—SRP covers losses, so every kill’s a payday!
