Cepheus Industry

Cepheus offers comprehensive industry manufacturing services in null-sec space. We specialize in producing a wide range of ships, modules, and structures to support our members and alliance operations. Below is an overview of our production capabilities:

Subcapital Ships

We manufacture all subcapital ship classes, including frigates, destroyers, cruisers, battlecruisers, battleships, and industrial ships. Our efficient production lines ensure quick turnaround for fleet needs.

Capital Ships

Our advanced facilities produce capital ships up to carriers and dreadnoughts, providing essential support for large-scale operations in null-sec.

Modules

We produce T1 and T2 modules, covering weapons, armor, shields, propulsion, and electronics to equip your ships with reliable gear.

Structures

Cepheus builds deployable structures for null-sec infrastructure, including citadels, engineering complexes, and refineries to establish and maintain our presence in space.
